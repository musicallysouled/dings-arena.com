TRAVEL FROM HOME

Sit at home and enjoy the outer world!


1.City Walks Live

This is a platform which makes you feel that you're walking around the city. There are many cities in the list and it aslo includes features such as walk in her shoes, day/night mode and shares quick facts of the selected city. Stay at home and feel like walking around the cities you've dreamt of!

2.Drive And Listen

This platform is a virtual drive through more than 50 cities around the world while listening to real-time local radio and the sounds of street life. It's in crystal clear 4K video. You can also change the speed of the vehicle you're driving in.

3.WindowSwap

This platform gives you a view from someone else's window anywhere across the globe. The real time sounds alongwith the 4K quality of the view enhances the experience.

4.Virtual Vacation

This website let's you travel 100+ cities around the world from the comfort of your home. You can go on a walking tour, driving tour, flying tour, road trips, enjoy features like live cam, monuments, videarth and city guesser. So if you feel bored and can't go out, enjoy this virtual platform!


Click here to visit site

5.Roundme

Select a location and get memerized in the environment. Roundme brings to you a platform where you choose a location and enjoy the 360 degree panoramic view. It's a website designed for fast, easy exploration and creation of virtual tours that use panoramic VR photography. Creators can upload their panoramic photos (15 per month with a free account) to create beautiful, immersive experiences that allow viewers to explore indoor and outdoor spaces in rich detail.


Click here to visit site

6.Airpano

AirPano is a VR project created by a team of Russian photographers focused on taking high-resolution aerial 360° photographs and 360° aerial panoromas. It has more than 4,200 panoramas of around 400 places of the Earth, including the North Pole, the Antarctic, volcano eruptions and even panoramas from the stratosphere, are presented on the website. Every week a new virtual tour is published on the website www.AirPano.com..
